Ingredients

  • 6 slices
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp.brown sugar
  • 1 tsp.vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp.cinnamon
  • 4 strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup rasberries
  • Maple syrup, to taste
  • Powdered sugar, to taste
